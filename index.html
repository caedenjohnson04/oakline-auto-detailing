What's Included

A Complete
Interior Detail

Every booking is a full interior detail — no upsells, no add-on charges. Here's exactly what Caeden takes care of on every single job.

Full Vacuum & Extraction

Every seat, floor mat, carpet, and trunk vacuumed thoroughly — including tight crevices around consoles, seat rails, and door pockets that most vacuums miss.

Steam Sanitization

High-temp steam cleaning kills bacteria, eliminates odors, and lifts embedded grime from upholstery and fabric surfaces without harsh chemicals.

Dashboard & Surface Wipe-Down

Dashboard, center console, door panels, cup holders, vents, and all hard surfaces cleaned and conditioned to restore a like-new look and feel.

Upholstery & Stain Treatment

Fabric seats, headliners, visors, and carpets treated for stains and soiling. Leather surfaces cleaned and conditioned to prevent cracking and fading.

Interior Glass Cleaning

Windshield, rear window, and all interior glass cleaned streak-free — the overlooked detail that makes a huge difference in how clean a car actually feels.

Final Walkthrough

Before wrapping up, every surface is inspected and touched up. If something isn't right, it gets addressed on the spot — no job is finished until it looks perfect.
